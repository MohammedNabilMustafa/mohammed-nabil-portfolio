# Project Brief — Mohammed Nabil Portfolio

## Overview

Build a premium personal portfolio website for **Mohammed Nabil**.

This is **not** a normal CV website. It should read and feel like a **one-person software consulting brand** — confident, professional, and enterprise-grade.

---

## Positioning

Mohammed Nabil is a **Business Systems Engineer** who builds custom ERP, CRM, Finance, Inventory, Manufacturing, AI, and Business Automation platforms for manufacturing companies.

---

## Case Studies

**Industry:** Manufacturing & Printing

#### Key Numbers

- 500+ active users

### Case Study 2 — Minsal Commerce OS

**Industry:** Modest Fashion / E-commerce

#### Key Numbers

- 81 pages/routes
- 135 React components

#### Technology Stack

## Important Notes

- Do **not** make it look like a normal CV website.
- Make it look like a **one-person software consulting brand**.
- Lead with business outcomes, systems thinking, and real-world impact — not a list of jobs or skills.
- **Ain Shams Press** is the primary manufacturing/ERP proof point.
- **Minsal Commerce OS** is the primary e-commerce/commerce operations proof point.
- Both case studies support the consulting brand narrative — real systems, real scale, real business impact.
